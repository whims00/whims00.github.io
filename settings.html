How it works

⌘ Mac users: Use Command (⌘) instead of Ctrl. Just hold ⌘ when recording.
⚡ Recording: Click the button, then press your desired key combo — it records automatically on keydown.
🔒 Avoid browser shortcuts like Ctrl+T, Ctrl+W, Ctrl+R or they won't work reliably.
💾 Auto-saved to your browser — settings persist across sessions.
